Peter Callesen

I'm sure you've seen Peter Callesen's paper art before but it's nice to have a permanent link to his website. His most iconic work involved cutting and folding a single sheet of A4 paper. Things that sound simple are often wonderfully complex. If you're interested - it's 80gsm paper, I don't know what stock!

Website Owners Manual

Paul Boag (founder of the agency Headscape) has released an excellent book targeted at all those responsible for a website - the owner, designers, developers, managers, marketers and everyone else along for the ride. The book is unique in that it looks at how to make the whole process successful. Especially useful for designers who want to communicate more effectively.
