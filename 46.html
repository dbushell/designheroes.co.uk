Stock Blend (beta)

Not to encourge the use of stock photography, but sometimes (most times) that's all the client will pay for. Stock Blend is an experimental search engine that returns photography from all the major players like iStockPhoto. Useful to see which galleries return good images and which return generic clichés, unless of course you're searching for a "business handshake". Then you're on your own.
