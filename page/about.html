About Design Heroes

Design Heroes was a blog and tag-based bookmark repository for all things art, design, photography and web development on the Internet. Whatever took our fancy really - from inspirational portfolios to must-read articles.


This website was preserved on 18th March 2012.

The Authors

David Bushell (@dbushell on twitter) & Ryan S. Young (@ryansyoung) co-created Design Heroes to replace their poor memory and terrible bookmarking skills. (Well David does the hard work, Ryan was just there in the pub when the idea came to life.)
