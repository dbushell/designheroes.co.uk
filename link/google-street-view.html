Google Street View

Posted by David Bushell

Google Street View stop-motion video produced by Sehsucht for agency Kolle Rebbe. Despite this amazing animation, Google's Street View isn't going down well in Germany!

This internet viral/information spot, done by our Berlin Team for the good folks at Kolle Rebbe takes the viewer on a journey around the world. In each chapter we see the most impressive things from google Streetview.

Read more at Sehsucht.de.

You can see the 4 week production side-by-side in this video: Google WIP 4 Weeks. It shows everything from the storyboards, 3D mock-ups and post-production effects.

Very cool.
