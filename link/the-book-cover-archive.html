Review

The Book Cover Archive

Posted by Ryan Young

The BCA is just one of the many good web ideas on the go from General Projects. Okay, so it's not a ground breaking idea. It's not going to change the world. But it is a great execution of a neat little idea. 

Straightforward, well presented and a clean look makes it a great archive of lots of different book covers; some good, some really good and some subject to personal taste. It's the simplicity of the site that makes it a winner. A layout that allows the book covers to speak for themselves, yet allowing you to go deeper and find out more on each cover if you want.

Not only a great resource but also a lovely visual zeitgeist. Worth sticking in your bookmarks.
