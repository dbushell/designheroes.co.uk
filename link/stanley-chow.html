Review

Stanley Chow

Posted by David Bushell

Stanley Chow is an illustrator from Manchester, UK. According to his US management agency Bernstein & Andriulli:

He began his career in 1997, mainly as a fashion illustrator and storyboard artist, and has become one of the most highly respected and established artists in the UK, working across a platform of media in advertising, design, publishing, packaging, gaming, animation and interactive.

And who are we to argue, his work is brilliant! I'm quite fond of his Mad Men series (which will come as no surprise to anyone who knows me). His caricature style captures the personalities perfectly. At £22 a print, I think I just might...
