The Digital Agencies of the Future!

Posted by David Bushell

"Showcasing the mobile websites of some of my favorite agencies (seriously)."

The Digital Agencies of the Future! is a somewhat unsurprising showcase of poor website design & development from some of the so called big 'digital' agencies. The sin they all share is 100% reliance of Flash instead of web standards, resulting in a rather embarrassing pubic display of "we don't know what we're doing".
