Review

xheight

Posted by David Bushell

xheight is a new blog about design ideas and thoughts.

xheight aims to share the inner-most secrets of professional designers. On one hand, this blog will allow its authors to understand their own discipline, but more importantly, it will allow others a new perspective on what design can mean.

This is a project I started several weeks ago and the reception has been very positive. The designers I've contact so far will be posting articles on the theory and philosophy of design and how it fits into their lifestyle. Essentially, what design means to them.

Follow updates on Twitter @xheightblog.
