Linotype: The Film Trailer

Posted by David Bushell

The Film is a documentary about Ottmar Mergenthaler's amazing Linotype typesetting machine and the people who own and love these machines today.

Linotype: The Film is being directed and edited by Douglas Wilson, a designer and film makers from midwest America. If it turns out to be anything like the Typeface Movie it will be a fascinating look into a dying art form. Or an hour long sleep-inducing eulogy, depending on your level of interest. I for one am excited.
