Where Are All the Ed-Ex Designers?

Posted by David Bushell

Where Are All the Ed-Ex Designers? An article by Khoi Vinh on the lack of editorial experience designers working today.

I’m talking about the kind of person who can build a great digital product out of great editorial content, a difficult enough challenge on its own.

The whole article is well worth a read. I've recently written a long piece about the failure of news online; it doesn't have to be the death of an industry. If more "ed-ex designers" appear on the horizon we're in for a revival.
