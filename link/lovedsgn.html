Review

LoveDsgn

Posted by David Bushell

Design Heroes has been somewhat quiet(er) lately and for good reason. We've been hard at work launching a new website: LoveDsgn.

LoveDsgn is a new social website for designers, typographers, all kinds of creative people. It's a platform for sharing, discussing and improving your creative output.

LoveDsgn is a great way for designers to get feedback and showcase their design work - and writing (on creativity). Unlike other "social portfolio" websites LoveDsgn actively encourages constructive feedback, and provides a large 640x360 canvas to display each post. The focus is on typography, but everything from photography to web design is welcome.

The site is currently in its infancy but already has a user base of over 300 passionate creative people. LoveDsgn is invitation only but all you need to do is register your interest. You can subscribe to the LoveDsgn RSS feed for alternative viewing pleasure, and follow on twitter @lovedsgn for the latest news.

Expect more unique features in the coming months.
