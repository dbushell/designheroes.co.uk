Review

Yiying Lu

Posted by David Bushell

Yiying Lu is a Chinese/Australian designer made famous for her illustration of the twitter fail whale. Her portfolio consists of similar illustration, graphic design, art direction and logos.

“Yiying” is 2 characters in Chinese. “Yi” means Happy; “Ying” means Creative.Yiying is an idea girl, a global soul, and multifaceted visual communicator with a unique blend of art & technology.

"Happy Creative" is definitely an apt name and it shows in her portfolio. The work maintains a level of sophistication while remaining colour and full of life. Compare the "Native Collection" illustrations to the ASDA wine bottles and you can see how a talented designer can find an appropriate style while maintaining that personal touch.

Very nice! Yiying Lu also blogs on posterous, and twitter obviously :)
