Graphic Design and Illustration is the official blog by The University of Cumbria, where I was taught everything I know!

Hello and welcome. This blog comes to you from the Graphic Design and Illustration courses at The University of Cumbria. From time to time we'll be posting student work and other things that catch our eye, both inside and outside the studio.

A blog to follow because in my (unbiased) opinion they deliver one of the best Graphic Design courses in the country, untarnished by the skinny jeans & fixie-riding of London.
