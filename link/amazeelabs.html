Review

AmazeeLabs

Posted by David Bushell

AmazeeLabs is a new web design and development agency based in Zurich, Switzerland. The agency has spun off of Amazee – "an internet platform that empowers individuals and organizations to initiate and promote their ideas and plans in groups, to find like-minded people and raise funds".

I don't know what that means, but I like their style. And the colourful and creative AmazeeLabs website is enough for me to forgive the use of "solutions" in a service title.

That’s right, from this day on you can buy our services in the fields of Community Solutions, Web Development and Web Consulting; basically, we’ll do everything that revolves around the Web and Social Media.

With such a stunning website and passionate introduction I get the sense this agency is one to keep an eye on in the future.
