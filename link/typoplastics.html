Review

Typoplastics

Posted by David Bushell

Typoplastics is a design experiment by Mitch Goldstein.

I will respond to these endless lists by creating my own, well designed list. A grouping of work that has a relationship and bearing on each other. A series of artifacts create by one individual (myself) based on a common content and impetus.

The resulting creations are a mix of typography and techniques like the use of photography, scanners, letraset and gouache, and they are quite simply a brilliant design series.

This is the sort of project every designer should be doing to master their craft.
