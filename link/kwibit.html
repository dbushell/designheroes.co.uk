Kwibit

Posted by David Bushell

Kwibit is a new website that lets you "collect and share your moments of inspiration" created by me! It's a tool to create quotes from any website, keep track of the inspiring things you've read, and share it all on Twitter - all in a simple click!

More information will be available in the coming days. In the meantime get your email address on the beta list as invites will be dished out very soon!
