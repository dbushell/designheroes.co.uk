Just My Type

Posted by David Bushell

Just My Type is a book of stories about fonts by Simon Garfield.

You can read my review on my personal blog at dbushell.com - highly recommended! (The book, but also my other blog).

Just My Type puts typography in context with a modern world, while seamlessly tying in the history and legacy that makes the subject wonderful.
