Adaptive Images

Posted by David Bushell

One of the downsides to serving the same website to all devices (which you should be doing) is that the same desktop-sized images are a very heavy burdon on mobile phones. 

Adaptive Images is a technique to detect device size and serve compressed versions.

Why? Because your site is being increasingly viewed on smaller, slower, low bandwidth devices. Your desktop-centric images load slowly, cause UI lag, and cost your visitors un-necessary bandwidth and money. You've implemented a responsive design in CSS, and need something to adapt your <img>'s too.

I suspect we'll hear a lot more in this space and new solutions and experiments unfold.
