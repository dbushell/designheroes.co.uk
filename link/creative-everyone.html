Creative Everyone

Posted by David Bushell

Creative Everyone is an online guide for creative events, brought to us by Daniel Howells.

We started Creative Everyone to be a single, go-to guide to all the most interesting creative-oriented events guide curated and edited by interested members from the creative community (our editors).

The site is US and UK centric and should prove to be a very handy resource. I'll be the first to admit despite living in London I don't visit nearly enough art & design events. Creative Everyone is bookmarked for future reference!
