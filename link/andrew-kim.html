Review

Andrew Kim

Posted by David Bushell

Andrew Kim is a Korean Canadian product designer. The portfolio on his blog Design Fabulous is very impressive, even more so when you noticed he's currently a 18 year old product design student at CCS (College for Creative Studies, Detroit USA).

His recent work "HTC 1" (pictured above, a concept for an Android mobile phone), demonstrates a level of skill and perfection that rivals what even Apple are producing for real.

Earlier this year Kim's work on redesigning the Coke bottle was rightly marveled at on most product design blogs - see the Eco Coke Bottle here. It's often the simplest of ideas that are the hardest to imagine and bring to life.

I see a bright future for this young designer!
