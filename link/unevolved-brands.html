Unevolved Brands

Posted by David Bushell

Unevolved Brands, “a study on brand and logo simplification” by ImJustCreative (Graham Smith). The idea is to abstract famous logos into basic circle designs, and see if they are still recognisable.

There are some clues along the way, but it's amazing just how many logos are too memorable to hide behind circles!

So some brands are easier than others, usually unique colours and round based logos are the easiest to decode. It’s an ongoing study, so new logos and brands will be added.

For alternate viewing you can follow the logo updates on Flickr.
