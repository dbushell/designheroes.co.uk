Aloha Editor

Posted by David Bushell

Aloha Editor:

The world's most advanced browser based Editor lets you experience a whole new way of editing. It’s faster than existing technologies and offers unprecedented functionalities.

Implementation technicalities aside, this type of product could revolutionize the web services industry. If you've every tried to design a website for a content managed system that clients are expected to update, you'll know the difficultly and frustration. Design is very limited by a text editor. I would sell my soul for something like Aloha!
