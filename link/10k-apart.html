10K Apart

Posted by David Bushell

23 days remain for An Event Apart's "10K Apart" - to inspire the web with just 10k. The challenge is simple:

It's time to get back to basics - back to optimizing every little byte like your life depends on it. Your challenge? Build a web app in less than 10 kilobytes.

The web app must be under 10kb (html, images, CSS and JavaScript all counted) and use appropriate web standards (read: no Flash). To make things fairer you're allowed to use a CDN-hosted version of jQuery or Prototype for free.

With over $6000 and registration to an AEA event at stake the prize is nothing to be sniffed at! To date there are 16 entries on the website. According to their twitter feed an FAQ will be online soon to answer more questions.

Time to get coding! Any ideas?
