QR Hacker

Posted by David Bushell

QR Hacker — Create custom QR codes with this web app by Carnation Group.

Useful, but it doesn't suggest where to actually use QR codes, and judging by the laughable executions we often see by clueless ad agencies today that sort of guide/warning would be beneficial! Pro tip: QR codes are suppose to make accessibility quicker and easier, not provide an additional barrier to content. URLs are perfectly adequate in most situations.
