How Low Can Your Logo?

Posted by David Bushell

How Low Can Your Logo? is an experiment testing the human capacity to willingly create that which you spend every day trying not to create: the worst logo ever.

I don't know about you but I fancy a go at this brief!

Bookmark for November 15 when "the client" will be announced. The deadline is the 8th December and the judges are a formiddable lineup including Bryony Gomez-Palacio and Armin Vit of UnderConsideration.

The effort will be painful and we will lose a lot of good designers in the process. The champion, however, will be revered as the logo lancelot with an epically bad logo to be feared by generations to come!
