Super Sexy CPR

"Sex sells." - Using sex in communications is not a particularly clever, or insightful method of drawing interest to a product or service, but it can be effective. The world is full of scantily clad men and women promoting all sorts of wares, the last thing we need is more. Yet, I can forgive this campaign promoting CPR & basic first aid. It's shot really well, has a great style and it works. My CPR skills are a lot better because of it.
