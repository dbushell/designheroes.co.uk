Up There

Up There is a documentary following the dying art of hand painted billboards in New York. Ignoring the Stella Artois sponsorship and product placement, it almost brings a tear to the eye. Considering the alternative, why are we not returning to this technique? Directed by Malcolm Murry based on concepts by Mother, NY.
