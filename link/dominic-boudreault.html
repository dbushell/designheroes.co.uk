Dominic Boudreault

Posted by David Bushell

Dominic Boudreault is a motion photographer with a stunning series of urban timelapse videos in high definition.

The expression “motion photographer” came to me spontaneously since movement is the basis of my work: I am constantly in search of a perspective that will transmit both the beauty and the speed of the world in which we live in.

Reminds me of Hayaku: A Time Lapse Journey Through Japan which Design Heroes featured last year. Always breathtaking, and almost makes a city look tranquil (almost).
