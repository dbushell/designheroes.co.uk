Review

Fudge

Posted by David Bushell

UK agency Fudge, based in Bolton, Greater Manchester, have launched their new website just in time for a prosperous 2011.

Our work has always been based on big ideas that engageconsumers with our client’s brand.The end goal should always beto find a way to get your customersto connect with you on a deeper level, not just providing a generic response to a client brief.

Fudge have been on my radar for a quiet a while because their "coming soon..." page has appeared on many design blogs over the last year. That's an impressive feat which of course raised high expectations for their actual website.

The new Fudge website takes on a fresher approach from the highly polished but somewhat constrained holding page layout. It's a beautifully curated portfolio that leaves you wanting more - a perfect place for a potential client to be.
