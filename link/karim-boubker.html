Review

Karim Boubker

Posted by David Bushell

Karim Boubker is a photographer and video artist based in Morocco producing work inspired by a great outlook on life.

I believe in three simple philosophies. To always live life to its fullest, to dream large and passionately, and to love endlessly. I believe that these are the true secrets for a life full of hope, happiness and contentment.

This philosophy very much comes out in his photography and should be an inspiration to all artists and designers, enjoy what you do!

I particularly like his video work which demonstrates a very strong professional and artistic eye for production. Video is an area I haven't touch on myself, that's probably why I find it most fascinating and inspiring.
