Review

The Mid-Century Modernist

Posted by David Bushell

I've recently come across The Mid-Century Modernist - a website dedicated to the inspiration of the modernist art movement between 1945 and 1970.

This site is a tribute to the mid-century modern movement as both a historical milestone and a living ideal, reflected in today’s best furniture, architecture, and design.

The Mid-Century Modernist was created by Stephen Coles (also of Typographica) an American graphic designer. With a team of editors, Coles delivers a humbling and nostalgic slice of modernist life reflected in today's design.

Redesigned in June 2010, it really is a fantastic website and presented absolutely beautifully. The charcoal and bright accent colours create a perfect atmosphere for showcasing design. The grid layout is reminiscent of graphic design from the era.

Visit The Mid-Century Modernist, you won't leave for a few hours!
