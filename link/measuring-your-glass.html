Measuring Your Glass

Posted by David Bushell

Measuring Your Glass - an article on positive design thinking by Bill Kenney of Focus Lab. A few days ago I posted my own article on positive thinking and Bill expands on that idea with much more depth and understanding than I managed to achieve.

As designers we live for the idea of creativity without boundaries. Make sure not to create artificial boundaries for yourself through negativity. By choosing to not release negative barriers in a reasonable time frame, you are creating mental road blocks for yourself.

There are many ideas that as designers we should remind ourselves daily.
