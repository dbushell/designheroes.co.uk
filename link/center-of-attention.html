Review

Center of Attention

Posted by David Bushell

Center of Attention is a collection of record centre labels curated by Simon Foster.

Whilst record cover sleeve art has always recieved plenty of attention (and rightly so) I believe that center labels have been somewhat neglected, so I thought I would set up this simple and easy to use site so I could share some examples.

A nice little collection that might be useful to study when creating design and typography within difficult shapes. I'm just thankful the web isn't confined to such boundaries!
