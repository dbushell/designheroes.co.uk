icotrip - Each day a new (ico)trip. This is a pretty cool daily project by ...an anoymous designer it would appear!

Icotrip.com is like a road movie in the world of icons. Every day, you will discover a new (ico)trip experience! Don’t wait for a conventional icon because you will be disappointed! Icotrip is the world of the offbeat humour! Be strong, I trust you dear visitor…

They come with a Creative Commons attribute non-commercial license, which means they could be useful to you. Not me, but I think it's a great personal project, for whoever it is.
