Review

Ryan Singer at Future of Web Apps, London 2010

Posted by David Bushell

Ryan Singer at Future of Web Apps, London 2010. Ryan Singer is the product manager and UI designer at 37signals. It's always nice to see how other people work, both as a learning experience and a bit of self confirmation that you're doing it "right".

In this talk, I walk through the steps of creating a web app including modeling, sketching, HTML, Photoshop explorations, and moving from static mockups to live running code.

If you couldn't afford the jaw dropping price of a FOWA London ticket this year (like me) you'll be please to see some of the talks appearing online.
