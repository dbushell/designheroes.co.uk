Banksy does Simpsons Intro

Posted by David Bushell

The Simpsons jumped the shark years ago and these days have to resort to celebrity cameos to increase viewing figures (because it sure isn't the humour). Their latest appearance is none other than the only graffiti artist anyone knows - Banksy. The intro quickly descends into the dark truth of corporate America, highlighting slave labour with enough unicorns to get it past an ignorant review board and audience. Classic Banksy.
