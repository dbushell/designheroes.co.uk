Review

Pedro Vidotto

Posted by David Bushell

If you were to do a quick Google search for "minimal movie posters" you'll quickly see how popular this concept genre is with designers. It's up there with Helvetica calendars and Pantone branded products. For that reason many designers will scoff at the idea, calling it cliche and tired. While it's true that the idea can't be called original anymore I think the practice of "having a go" at a popular design trend is commendable.

The challenge presents something different because of its popularity. You're competing against so many similar designs you really need to stand out. You have to one-up what's been done before with your own personal touch. If you're successful it's a massive achievement. I think Pedro Vidotto's minimal posters are superb. They have their own aesthetic that stays true for all 40+ of them - yes that many!

And the photography/artworking of them is a nice touch. Presentation of design is everything. I think I need to start photographing my design work on a wooden service. It certainly beats bull-dog clips or the classic "poster pose".
