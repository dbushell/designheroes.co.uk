Creative Boom Relaunches

Posted by David Bushell

Creative Boom relaunches with a brand new design! If you've never visited – Creative Boom is an online magazine network & community that supports the creative industry. It includes regional hubs that showcase creatives in over 20 cities across the UK. Each hub is curated by a regional editor. The network is run by Boomerang, a PR Agency is Cheshire.

We really love the end result and we hope you do too. This new design splits our content into four main sections: Inspire; Social; Work and Fun. The idea being - to make things simple and easy to navigate around.

The site is a fantastic source for exploring the UK creative scene. They also host Social Boom networking events in several cities on a monthly basis. I'm a big fan, and the new Creative Boom website design is great fun.
