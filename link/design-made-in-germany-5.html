Review

Design Made in Germany 5

Posted by David Bushell

Dmig5 is the 5th edition of an online design magazine available in both PDF and an absolutely glorious HTML version. Some information (with the help of Google translate):

The Magazine of Design made in Germany presents projects of German designers and agencies, and offers articles on a different focal topic. It is free to the reader how he wants to "consume the magazine:" Whether it is optimized as an HTML version of the iPhone, or to print PDF magazine.

The web version linked here is a stunning piece of editorial web design. Most of the content is in Germany but worth browsing even if you struggle with the language. The magazine does contain the "The Grid System" article by Antonio Carusone of AisleOne in English.

Design Made in Germany is at the forefront of interactive editorial design. Just imagine what they'll be doing on devices like the iPad in a few years. Something tells me Design Heroes is in need for a refresh!
