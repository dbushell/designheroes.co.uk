MyLifeListed launches

Posted by David Bushell

MyLifeListed is a simple and fun way to list, count and share your life experiences. The site has just launched today and you can follow updates on twitter @mylifelisted.

This is a website I personally helped design and build with Base Creative, and we're very glad to be part of the project!

By keeping on-going lists of your life experiences all in one place, look back over time and feel good about what you've done, when you've done it, where you've been and what you've achieved.

It provides a uniquely simple service that I believe many will find very useful. You can read more about the design process here, and make sure to sign up!
