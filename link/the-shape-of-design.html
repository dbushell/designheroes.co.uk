Review

The Shape of Design

Posted by David Bushell

The Shape of Design is Frank Chimero's talk about the design process at Build Conference 2010.

To really think about design, you need to learn and think about everything other than it. Design is a vessel: the most important part is what it holds.

This is a refreshing talk to watch from a very articulate and experienced designer. Frank Chimero has recently launched an already successful Kickstarter project to fund and publish a book on these ideas.

I've spent the better part of the last two years writing and speaking on design and thinking about the topics that orbit the practice: storytelling, concept, craft, and improvisation. I want to take all of the ideas I've had and connected these past few months and capture them in a book format.

I have pledge my support and am looking forward to the book :)
