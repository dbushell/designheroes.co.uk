Time You Enjoy Wasting

Posted by David Bushell

Time You Enjoy Wasting is a limited edition poster by Ross Moody (@55his).

The simple truths are the best. This quote was said and re-said dozens of times but I believe it was originated by Bertrand Russel. Plus, I think flip clocks are awesome.

This poster speaks for itself, but you should check out Ross' other work at 55 Hi's. His graphic design style pleases my taste very much! Colourful, minimal but complex, typographic and thoughtful.
