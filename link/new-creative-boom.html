Review

New Creative Boom

Posted by David Bushell

Creative Boom - the UK based creative industries network - updates again with a new design aimed at promoting more great content. The network has also changed to regional hubs rather than individual cities which I'd say is a smart move for maintainability.

Since our launch in July 2009, this is the fifth time we've changed the design but we really felt that it was necessary to make the site as good as possible, prioritising the content more than anything else while helping you to navigate quickly and easily around the entire site.

The new design is a very different approach from the September update, and without the shackles of WordPress it has a refreshingly clean layout. I like that the menu has been reduced to be less ambiguous and far more usable.

Though I wouldn't call black, white and Helvetica particularly "creative", the site shows off the featured artists rather than itself, and that's a good thing! Perhaps something the last design didn't do well.

I think overall an excellent change.
