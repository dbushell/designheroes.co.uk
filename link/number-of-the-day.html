Number of the Day

Posted by David Bushell

Number of the Day is for the aesthetic appreciation of numerals, number systems and their uses, co-curated by Craig Wills and Randi Hazan.

If you like graphic design and you like numbers, Number of the Day is the blog for you! From 0–999, from the 20th to the 21st century, this is more numbers than you can probably count. Whatablog!

In all seriousness, this is a really amazing resource.

If you're more of an alpha than a numeric try the author's other blog TwentySixTypes.
