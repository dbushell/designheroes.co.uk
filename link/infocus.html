In:Focus - A series of brief questions with the web’s best…

in:focus aims to get to know these people a little more with insights into the lives of the people behind the posts. Each short interview will include a series of questions specifically designed to get more of an idea of the people we rely on and listen to so heavily.

Launched by Harry Roberts, a young and talented web developer based in the UK, In:Focus aims to be a high quality Q&A with the industries elite.

His first guest Oliver Reichenstein (CEO of Information Architects) sets the bar high and ensures this is a blog to bookmark.
