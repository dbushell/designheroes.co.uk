Design Heroes

Typographic Conundrums by Harry Pearce

Not often I feature a book on Design Heroes but this one is a "must have". I remember being lucky enough to sit in an audience listening to Harry Pearce talk about his design career. He projected some of these typographic conundrums on the wall to our delight. The first question in the Q&A session was "are you publishing them?". The answer was "no". Well that was a few years ago, guess Pearce changed his mind for the better!
