Color vs. Colour - The Great Spelling Battle

Posted by David Bushell

Color vs. Colour - The Great Spelling Battle is a fascinating article on the history and modern usage of the word, from the ColourLovers blog.

Being decidedly British I would obviously opt for the 'our' spelling, but being a web designer I'm forced to write with the 'or' variation in CSS. Here's something to think about:

When we first created COLOURlovers we used the British spelling throughout the site... but overtime we realized we were being penalized by the search engines, so we switched over to using the American spelling.

If your online product or service can cater to an international audience, perhaps the American spelling should be favoured after all.
