Review

Nanamee

Posted by David Bushell

Nanamee is a community based royalty free stock media library created by Michael Paul Young, launched back at the start of February.

So what does all that mean for you? If you’re a contributor, it’s somewhere you can sell your work alongside a community of world-class peers, with the unique niche of curation by a wold-class design brand.

Nanamee is a beautiful website and appears to be a great source of less traditional stock photography, which can only be a good thing. The social elements like 'following' aren't very prominant which doesn't help with the community feel, perhaps also the simplicity and cleanness of layout is counteractive to this aim. Regardless, it's a refreshing project with a quality that outshines the bigger libraries.
