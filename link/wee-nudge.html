Review

Wee Nudge

Posted by David Bushell

Wee Nudge is a collection of articles to teach your clients about the mysteries of the web.

Trying to convince a client not to squeeze everything above the fold? Is your whitespace filling up fast? We've collected a variety of articles on some sticky web subjects that might just help you make your point. Send your client to one of our topic pages for a quick intro, some links and a wee nudge in the right direction.

I applaud this effort, and while I don't think clients should really be asked to read articles, it will give you the ammunition needed to articulate a point.

Don't allow poor design choices to be made through your own inability!
