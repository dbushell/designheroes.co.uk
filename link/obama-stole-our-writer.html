Agency POKE are on the look out for a new copywriter after Obama stole their last one.

So, if you think you could be our new copywriter – and have no intentions of being seduced by a handsome President – please respond to the five challenges below...

POKE sure know how to recruit.

This is just as intriguing as their previous site, which surely had a lot of interest. This sort of ad inspires the truly great creatives - would you really go through a recruitment agency, or respond to an initiative like this? I know which one I'd prefer.
