Review

The LOVE Advent Calendar

Posted by David Bushell

Manchester based agency LOVE launch their interactive QR code advent calendar. Out all of the ones I've seen today this is by far the most creative.

They’ve been fly posted around Manchester and London, so if you spot one, make sure to scan the relevant code and check out what’s behind the door. There’s an online version too, which involves good old fashioned clicking instead of scanning.

Kudos for the innovation!
