Flaunt

Flaunt (subtitled "Designing effective, compelling and memorable portfolios of creative work") is a book from Under Construction authors Bryony Gomez-Palacio and Armin Vit. "Through more than forty case studies, nearly twenty interviews with experienced professionals, and a series of (nonscientific) surveys, Flaunt is a resource for design students as well as young, experienced, freelance, and independent designers."
