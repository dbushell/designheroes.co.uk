Review

Whale & Pine

Posted by David Bushell

Whale & Pine - Handpicked Art, Prints, T-Shirts, Totes and Accessories created by selected artists, illustrators and designers.

The site was founded by designer Camilo Bejarano and now hosts work of just over a dozen other artists and illustrators (some of whom have already been featured on Design Heroes like Paul Tebbott).

Whale and Pine set out to connect with the best and most talented artists, contemporary character designers and illustrators from around the world.

What sets this website apart from other similar ventures is the carefully curated nature of selecting highly talented individuals and providing their designs on different products. At the moment prints are in most abundance and they're all absolutely stunning.

I just love seeing independent artistic groups like Whale & Pine.
