Foundation

Posted by David Bushell

Foundation is the new video podcast and premium newsletter by Digg founder and web entrepreneur Kevin Rose.

Foundation is a monthly private email newsletter that features video interviews, product reviews, rants, and early access to pre-launched websites.

The newsletter is $3.99/month but the content will be free a week later, although considering how fast-paced web development is the early access could be a good deal. Foundation 001 is available on YouTube as embedded above.

In this series premiere of Foundation, Kevin Rose interviews Jack Dorsey, the creator, co-founder and chairman of Twitter and the CEO of Square.
