Review

Poke

Posted by David Bushell

Poke – the London based agency who specialised in online campaigns – have just updated their website.

The site focuses strongly on the blog and award winning portfolio. The new design works perfectly. It doesn't try to be overly flashly like most agency websites. It's simply clean, functional, and accessible.

The mobile version is something special. Browse on your iPhone and it'll immediately tell you have far away you are...

"You're 2.4km away from Poke"

... following by a map link, their phone number and email address.

A perfect example of considering your users and the content they're looking for (and not giving them your "mission statement"). Bravo!
