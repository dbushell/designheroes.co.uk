Review

Wez Maynard

Posted by David Bushell

Wez Maynard is a UK based designer and illustrator who specialises in website and UI design. This month he relaunched his personal website with a beautiful new design. It mixes a strong aesthetic style with an unusual layout. In Maynard's own words:

"Its taken *roughly* around 2 years, 24 months, 720 days, 17,280 hours, 1,036,800 minutes and 62,208,000 seconds in the making – as well as the last 10 extra minutes to work that post padding arithmetic out – but I finally have my site alive and breathing once again."

And without doubt it was worth the effort!

The design challanges normal convention by aligning itself right and makes it look effortless (believe me it's not an easy task!). The typography and grid structure are top notch, display solid graphic design principles – something that's often lacking on the web.

All in all a great achievement by Wez Maynard.
