Review

Foundation (new)

Posted by David Bushell

I've been watching Kevin Rose's Foundation since the very first episode. It's an incredibly inspiring series that I would reccomend to all website creators.

The new site matches the high production values of the videos perfectly. While I think the small screen design changes are a bit of a cop-out, you'll want to be watching these episodes in full HD anyway. Save them for the big screen, a quiet morning and a cup of tea. Remember why you enjoy working online.
