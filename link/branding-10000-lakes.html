Branding 10,000 Lakes

Posted by David Bushell

Branding 10,000 Lakes is a project by Nicole Meyer.

Lake logos have a tendency to be, well, fairly ugly. This project was created to rethink what they could be.One Minnesota Lake. One Logo. Every day. Should only take a little over 27 years to hit 'em all. Stay tuned and enjoy!

It's been a while since I've posted a purely design project on Design Heroes (my rules) so this one had to be something special. I just love these self initiated projects. The chance for experimentation in typography, graphics and branding is perfect for training the creative mind. If you're a designer without a project, initiate one now!
