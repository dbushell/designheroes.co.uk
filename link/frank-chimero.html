Frank Chimero

Frank Chimero is an illustrator from Portland, Oregon. Early on he was inspired by the likes of Saul Bass and Alan Fletcher. These sources have shaped his unique illustration style. His website and blog are also an excellent example of what a designers website should be, very nice indeed!
