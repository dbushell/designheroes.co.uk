Review

Bethany Heck

Posted by David Bushell

Bethany Heck is a recent Graphic Design graduate from Auburn University (Alabama).

Bethany enjoys web design, vintage typography, wood type, long strolls on the beach & speaking in the third person. Also, Typewriters.

It's not often designers manage to achieve a style equal to what inspires them, but I think Bethany's portfolio is strong enough to stand on its own, while drawing from a rich history of American graphic design.
