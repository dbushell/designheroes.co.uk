Khoi Vinh: On the Grid

Posted by David Bushell

The Color Machine sit down with Khoi Vinh to "discuss the subject that has made his work most noteworthy: the grid."

Color Machine collaborators Jordan Alport and Raafi Rivero produced this piece, which was directed by Raafi. The interview was filmed at our Brooklyn studio, while the many grid-like structures of New York City serve as visual proxy for grids on the web and everywhere else.

Make five minutes to watch this one. Vinh is an interesting guy, and the production of the video is something to study too.
