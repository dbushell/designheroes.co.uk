Review

JS1k

Posted by David Bushell

Last week Design Heroes featured the JavaScript challenge 10k Apart. In a similar spirit here is a considerably more gruelling challenge - the JS1k contest.

Though both challenges are fairly similar in concept the actual results are very different. 10k Apart focuses on building useful proof-of-concept style web apps. The 1k is a real hackers challenge showing off some insane optimization techniques. Many of the entries are 3D visual animations using canvas and a lot of complex maths.

The deadline is the 10th September 2010 and the prizes aren't too shabby. I'll probably stick to the 10k Apart challenge, just need to find the time to do something now!
