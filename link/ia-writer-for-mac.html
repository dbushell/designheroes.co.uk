Review

IA Writer for Mac

Posted by David Bushell

IA Writer for Mac - A minimalist word processor for Mac from Information Architects.

A better tool doesn’t make a better craftsman, but a good tool makes working a pleasure. IA Writer for Mac is a digital writing tool that makes sure that all your thoughts go into the text instead of the program.

I've been playing around with IA Writer all night and it's beautiful! The people at IA really know how to create an interface for designers. Does the minimalism improve writing? Who knows, but I'll be finding that out very soon.

IA Writer for Mac defines simplicity; something that every user interface needs but most fail to achieve. It removes the need for a mouse, the UI chrome, even the typeface options. The intro video takes a jab at Microsoft Word which is unsurprising, though slightly off colour.

I'm not afraid to admit I got suckered into a product designed for designers – god knows how many posters and prints I have still tubed in my cupboard – but I think I'll be giving this one a strong trial run, I think I enjoy it very much.

You can follow Information Architects @ia and @iawriter on Twitter.
