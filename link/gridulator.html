Gridulator

Posted by David Bushell

Gridulator – Make pixel grids, lickety-split.

Here's a neat little "web app" that calculates grid columns and gutters. If you're like me, doing any kind of maths is difficult (even with a calculator) so I applaud the efforts of Stuntbox, the New York design studio behind this online gadget.

One wishes for a "Create PSD" option with guidelines and all, but that's probably asking a bit too much. The keyboard shortcuts are a nice touch.
