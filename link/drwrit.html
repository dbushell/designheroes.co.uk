Review

drwr.it

Posted by David Bushell

drwr.it is a graphic design & illustration inspiration website created by Paul Scrivens' agency Emersian. Scrivens is of particular interest because he also created the well respected design discussion site Drawar.

drwr.it is a community where everyone finds and bookmarks the graphic design and illustrations that they like and share it with the community.

The site allows members to use a JavaScript bookmark to post inspiration from around the web, in a similar vein to Pinterest. Where it differs is its disinterest in the social aspect that many sites feel the need to incorporate these days.

I would normally cry at the use of Courier as a font choice in web design (or any design, for that matter) but drwr.it manages to pull off an underground, sub-culture style that reminds me of the early Internet. And for that I love it!

These type of sites will always be judged by the quality of work on display. drwr.it is currently showcasing some stunning illustration and seems to be curated to a high standard. Well worth a browse.
