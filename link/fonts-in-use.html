Review

Fonts in Use

Posted by David Bushell

Fonts in Use (@fontsinuse) is a design and typography blog cataloguing fonts in use.

So much of design critique is focused on graphics and photography. It’s time to shed light on the most basic element of communication: the type. At Fonts In Use we’ll catalog and examine real-world typography wherever it appears...

Outstanding effort by the creators Sam Berlow, Stephen Coles, and Nick Sherman. The site is very new so as an "inspiration" feed for individual fonts it's a bit sparse, but that's not the point. Each post goes into great depth with multiple photos, analysis and discussion of usage.

Design blogging at its highest quality.
