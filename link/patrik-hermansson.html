Review

Patrik Hermansson

Posted by David Bushell

Patrik Hermansson is a 17 year old photographer from Sweden. His new photo blog suggests a very promising talent is emerging onto the scene. I asked Patrik what his attitude towards photography was, and to tell us about the style he sets out to achieve:

I like to explore new sides to photography. Actually what's most important about my photography isn't the photographs. It's the experience taking them. When I hold a camera I see the world differently, more vividly. You become more observant about the small things around you, when you are holding a camera, that you wouldn't notice if you were just walking down the street. You are more present and your eyes are wide open.

What I found most interesting was his thoughts on interaction with other people and how that manifests within the photo. This is a great mindset to approach photograph:

Photography also makes me approach people I wouldn't have talked to otherwise. I always try to give my pictures a "human" feel. Because people tell stories, even if they are silent or not in the picture at all, a picture of a place where people have been also tell a story.

Keep up the good work Patrik!
