Peter Callesen

I'm sure you've seen Peter Callesen's paper art before but it's nice to have a permanent link to his website. His most iconic work involved cutting and folding a single sheet of A4 paper. Things that sound simple are often wonderfully complex. If you're interested - it's 80gsm paper, I don't know what stock!
