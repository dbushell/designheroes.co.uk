Review

Retinart

Posted by David Bushell

Retinart is the blog of Alex Charchar, an Australian graphic designer with a passion for exploring and reflecting upon creativity.

"With Retinart, what I'm doing is telling you about those things I read about. Those things that I'm still hungry to learn more about. Those things that give beauty to the science and science to the beauty."

The blog dropped of the radar for quite some time last year but has recently been revived with a brand new look and more outstanding content. Take some time to read the articles and you'll understand why it's not just another blog of design-likes. Retinart really sums up what a great design blog should be like. It's personal, it's full of design knowledge and research, and it really gives you something to reflect upon.

"In an effort to offer you great, new content, I often have to spend more than a day or two researching — a few of my articles might take a week or two instead. From this I might only get one or two articles, but it means that I haven't limited the content to what I already know and, hopefully, will be delivering something that isn't often found online."

As a website design itself the aesthetic style of Retinart is complex but consistently unique. It avoids the cliches of modern glossy design blogs and for that earns a place in the visitors memory. The typography is thoroughly appreciated and deserves a click out of your RSS reader whenever an new article is posted.

Be sure to check out Retinart often for the great articles.
