NO!SPEC To educate the public about speculative (spec) work.

Spec work and spec-based design contents are a growing concern. So in an effort to educate those working in the Visual Communication industry as well as the clients who use their services, a group of designers banded together to bring the NO!SPEC Campaign to the public.

Now here is a campaign I can get behind.
