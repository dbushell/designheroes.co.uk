Review

Don't Fear the Internet

Posted by David Bushell

Don't Fear the Internet is a serious of videos introducing basic HTML & CSS for non-web designers by Jessica Hische & Russ Maschmeyer.

Are you a print designer, photographer, fine-artist, or general creative person? Do you have a shitty website that you slapped together yourself in Dreamweaver in that ONE web design class that you took in college? Do you not have a site at all because you’ve been waiting two years for your cousin to put it together for you? Well, we’re here to help.

I've watched the first few videos and they're very well executed. Definitely an accessible resource for those looking to understand the web.

I think every graphic designer working today needs to know these basics. Even if you're print-based, the boundaries are always going to be crossed.
