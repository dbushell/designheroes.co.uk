Review

Horrible Logos

Posted by David Bushell

Horrible Logos is a design service where you pay 5 bucks for a logo fit for the beermat it was scribbled on and the designer gets another drink. It's a genius idea. I'm partciuarly fond of this paragraph from the terms of service:

Upon completion of the logo you will not receive any files. If you like the logo, I’m sorry. I will not redo the logo to make it look worse. I don’t accept any revision requests. The first design is also the final design. If you want the logo to use elsewhere, right click the one that I post on the website and save it to your desktop.

I think we all have something to learn here...
