When Facebook becomes a book

Posted by David Bushell

When Facebook becomes a book is an ad campaign for Bouygues Telecom, a French mobile provider. The video was created by Perfect Fools for DDB Paris.

They wanted us to create something that would go beyond using your profile picture in a funny way, or pranking your friends with a small joke.

I'm not a huge fan of Facebook but this video does give a nice nostalgic and warm feel to using the site. And it has proved to be a great campaign for Bouygues Telecom with apparently all 1000 limited edition personalised books being snapped up.
