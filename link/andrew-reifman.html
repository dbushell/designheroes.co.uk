Review

Andrew Reifman

Posted by David Bushell

Andrew Reifman is a graphic designer from Atlanta, Georgia. He graduated from the University of Georgia in 2007 (Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design) and now works for MailChimp - a web designers favourite!

His portfolio has a nice mix of branding, graphics and website design. Most designers trained in the traditional print-based arts often lack the talent to bridge the gap between graphic design and web design ("there's a difference?"). Andrew Reifman shows off superb ability to do both. Knowledge of web standards and coding skill are the key to this success.
