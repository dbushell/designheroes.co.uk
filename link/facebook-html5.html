Facebook HTML5

Facebook know what's good for the web, at least its technologies! I refuse to accept Facebook's idea of a social layer, but that's obviously just me.

With animation, offline capabilities, audio and more, HTML5 yields a new class of web standards enabling developers to build amazing products.

If Facebook are inevitably going to take over the world (I hope not) they've done the decent thing and gone standards-friendly. Thanks, I guess!
