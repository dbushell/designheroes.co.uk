Future Friendly

Posted by David Bushell

Future Friendly is a microsite by web industry leaders, including Jeremy Keith and Luke Wroblewski.

We want to make things that are future friendly. By anticipating what's next, we can react to today's concerns but also build long-term value for people and businesses. The following ideas have been on our minds recently. Help us explore them further or suggest new ones.

Future Friendly puts the web and its future into context. It suggests a pragmatic mindset for those designing, developing and producing websites. One that will ensure accessible content in years to come.
