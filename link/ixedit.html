IxEdit

IxEdit brings the power of coding jQuery into an easy to use graphical interface. It calls itself "The First On-the-Fly Interactive Design Tool for the Web" and although that sentence is a load of nonsense, the tool does look quite useful. (Especially for people who have never coded with jQuery.) And please, remember to remove IxEdit before deploying your website! Considering JavaScript "apps" are all the rage right now I'm looking forward to seeing more experimental stuff like this. In fact I'm actually working on one myself ;-)
