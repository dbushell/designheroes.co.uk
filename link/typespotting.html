Review

Typespotting

Posted by David Bushell

Typespotting is a new typography blog by Mattia Forza (@UniqueDesignIT) an Italian graphic designer.

A simple blog featuring typography-focused photographs taken around the towns I visit.

I love this blog concept because it promotes the idea of finding inspiration everywhere and keeping the senses open to design at all times. To be a graphic designer you must understand the cultural effect it has in the real world, not just on a Mac screen :)

Who doesn't love hand-painted typography? I might just take my camera for a strolll this weekend.
