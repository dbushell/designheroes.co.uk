Review

Typenuts

Posted by David Bushell

If you like typography and you love decorating your screen background - Typenuts is for you. The site is brought to us by I Love Typography.

Type-themed & type-inspired iPhone & desktop wallpapers for typenuts. Completely free; no strings attached.

Generally I don't like to feature design galleries on DH that provide little more than a continues stream of regurgitated content - there's enough of those online already - but Typenuts seems to be carefully curated, high quality and type-specific.

And we all love that.
