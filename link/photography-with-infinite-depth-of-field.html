Photography with Infinite Depth of Field

Posted by David Bushell

"Adobe demos photography with infinite depth of field" – amazing new technology shown in this article over at Business 2.0 Press – not a source I frequent often... The product was demonstrated at a recent Nvidia Tech Conference.

The company used plenoptic lenses, which essentially are many lenses clustered together that are able to focus on different parts of a picture (including the ability to capture 3D images), the result is a mosaic like non-natural group of micro-pictures with an infinite depth of field.

It's both hardware and software (and very expensive) so don't expect the feature to pop up in Photoshop anytime soon. See YouTube for a live presentation clip. I get the feeling photographers are going to start disliking Adobe more and more in years to come!
