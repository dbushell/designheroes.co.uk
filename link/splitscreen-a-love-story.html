Splitscreen: A Love Story

Posted by David Bushell

Splitscreen: A Love Story. This is a short HD film shot with a Nokia N8 mobile phone, directed by JW Griffiths. It won the Nokia Shorts competition at the Edinburgh Film Festival 2011. Make sure to watch the making of too.

In it the duo go to Paris to shoot some cool locations on the Nokia N8. They encounter accordion players, dodgy bikes and get up to all kinds of Steadicam adventures.

Now where can I buy a steadicam for my iPhone?
