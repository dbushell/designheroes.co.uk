Awwwards

Posted by David Bushell

The CSS Awards book featuring the Site of the Day through 2010.

The AWWWARDS were created to acknowledge the creativity, innovation and talent among the best designers, studios, and agencies of the world through their websites. All of them are selected for the Site of the Day and Site of the Month by our international panel of judges using a strict valuation system under the criteria of design, creativity, content, and usability.

I bought the book because my design portfolio & blog won Site of the Day this summer! I'm Mr 8th July I think, although I didn't win the monthly award.

It will be very exciting to see myself in print. Not to flatter myself, but the quality of the other websites featured at The CSS Awards is incredibly high so this book should prove a great guide to 2010 in web design.
