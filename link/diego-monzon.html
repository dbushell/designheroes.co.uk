Review

Diego Monzon

Posted by David Bushell

Diego Monzon is a freelance user interface designer.

Schooled in the fine arts, Diego Monzon has over 10 years of experience as a freelance designer. Although he is passionate about all aspects of design, he has developed a knack for creating attractive user interfaces.

You can see his work at diegomonzon.com (coming soon), LoveDsgn, Graphic River, and PSDtuts+ and of course follow the latest on twitter @userdiegomonzon.

His design style is as realistic as it comes. Shadows, highlights and textures play a big role in his work, and the level of detail and finish is incredible. Diego Monzon is setting a very high standard for all UI designers.

Great work!
