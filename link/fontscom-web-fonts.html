Fonts.com Web Fonts

Posted by David Bushell

Monotype Imaging Announces the Commercial Launch of Fonts.com Web Fonts. You can read the enthralling press release here.

The new service makes available (for web use) over 7500 fonts from the MonoType, LinoType and ITC collections. It will compete with other services already online such as Typekit, WebINK and Webtype (from Ascender Corporation and friends).

This is by far the biggest collection of commercial web fonts to be made available through one service and a big play to dominate this market. I've discussed web font pricing before and while I won't be opening my wallet just yet, this new service is at least competitively priced.

There's an interesting article over at Mashable about this launch. Ignoring the ridiculous title, the article does contain this gem:

Vice President of Marketing Chris Roberts told me that Monotype doesn’t want to risk being passed over simply because it’s not willing to embrace the future. That’s one reason he said Monotype is so enthusiastic about the future of web fonts.

Cutting edge stuff. Grudgingly copy the competition and hang along for the ride. At least they're making a play now though, and that's what we asked for.
