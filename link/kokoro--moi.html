Review

Kokoro + Moi

Posted by David Bushell

When I first landed on the new Kokoro + Moi home page I automatically assumed this website was built with Adobe Flash. I was pleasantly surprised therefore when I noticed it was pure HTML (with JavaScript for interactivity). Perhaps even more impressive is that it's built on top of the WordPress blogging platform. But back to Flash:

100% Flash websites seem to be ever common amongst high-end creative agencies. A need to stand out as more "creative" than other agencies tends to produce all sorts of interactive ideas that will "break the mould" of traditional website design. Unusual interactivity it not necessarily good or bad and each implementation should be judged on it's own merit. More often than not this added "creativity" leads to slow loading, unusable scrolling concepts and ultimately a quick exit for the visitor.

So props to Kokoro + Moi for developing their website the "right way".
