CreativeRoots

Posted by David Bushell

The global design archive CreativeRoots updates with a new website design.

As you may know, over the past two years CreativeRoots has built up an impressive art and design collection based on countries of the world, with each post being influenced by its countries, culture and history. I decided that it is about time to make this blog more attractive and user-friendly.

The new design follows a strong grid structure, with mutliple views for grid and list style. It also introduces a bit of keyboard navigation! (did you know you can browse Design Heroes with the up and down arrows?).

You can follow @creativeroots on Twitter.
