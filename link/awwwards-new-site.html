Review

Awwwards (new site)

Posted by David Bushell

"The CSS Awards" re-brands as the Awwwards with a new site for 2011.

Awwwards are the awards that recognize and promote the talent and effort of the best developers, designers and web agencies in the world.

After a very successful 2010 it's good to see this site refreshed for a new round. Removing "CSS" from the name is a smart move to differentiate itself from the hundreds of generic "CSS galleries" on the web. Awwwards in curated by a highly talented team.

I was honoured back in July but looking at the current standard I think it's time to up my game! New personal website for the summer? Maybe...
