Review

CubeDude

Posted by David Bushell

CubeDude is a cute collection by Angus MacLane:

Presenting the CubeDude: a new scale for figure building. All of the charm of Miniland scale with the specificity of the Minifig scale.All models by Angus MacLane, all photos by Scott Clark (sclarky on Flickr).

Be sure to check out the CubeDude Workshop photo collection for a peek at the development process. He has it down to an art form! No surprise that Angus MacLane is a Pixar animator and has worked on everything from Toy Story to WALL-E.

CubeDude's have cheered my morning right up!
