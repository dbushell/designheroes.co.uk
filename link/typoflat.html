TypoFlat

Posted by David Bushell

TypoFlat is a personal & experimental project of designer Branislav S. Cirkovic (@brancirkovic).

The idea is to have this free flow of creation where no clients or money are involved, just a pure passion for creation and form experimentation. Not every character, image or vector is perfect so please feel free to take full liberty and adjust it so it fits your needs, experiment and never forget to enjoy the process!

This is artistic expression and graphic design at its purest and the results are great. I always say every designer should experiment outside of client work (not that I do enough). That's where the real magic happens.
