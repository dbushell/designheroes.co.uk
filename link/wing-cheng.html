Review

Wing Cheng

Posted by David Bushell

Wing Cheng is a graphic designer and web designer from the Netherlands.

His personal website is beautiful, original and an all round amazing bit of creativity. While it doesn't strictly adhere to web standards and practices, it does prove there are no definitive rules in web design & development. For a designer's portfolio impressive visuals are priority and Wing Cheng pulls this off spectacularly.

There are a few tweaks that could be easily made to improve the site from a standards point of view but ultimately very few people would notice - and that's a perfectly fine place to be.
