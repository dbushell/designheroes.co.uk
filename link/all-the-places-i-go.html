Review

All The Places I Go

Posted by David Bushell

All The Places I Go is an experimental project looking at social geo-tagging and check-ins by Simon Elvery.

Check-ins are quite isolated pieces of data; there are huge gaps in both the record, and types of analysis that can be performed. It's difficult or impossible to expose some of the facts that may be of most interest.

This is a facsinating project to follow because it's taking ideas started by the likes of FourSquare and Gowalla, and pushing them to the next level. The site is updated regularly with fresh data and new ways to visualise it.
