A LIST APART Survey, 2009

Posted by David Bushell

Findings from the A LIST APART Survey, 2009.

Once again, A List Apart and you have teamed up to shed light on precisely who creates websites. Where do we live? What kind of work do we do? What are our job titles? How well or how poorly are we paid? How satisfied are we, and where do we see ourselves going?

Very interesting information!

Over 50% of participants have a university degree (or better), and around the same percentage think that education holds very little relevance. My opinion has always been that a degree is just a golden ticket into a job interview. A solid portfolio and a few years experience is worth a lot more. I'm still proud of my BA (Hons) though!
