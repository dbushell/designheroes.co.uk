Dana Tanamachi

Posted by David Bushell

Dana Tanamachi (@dana_tanamachi) is a graphic designer and custom chalk letterer living in Brooklyn, New York.

After hours, she can be found writing on people's walls all over New York City as a custom chalk letterer.

Incredible talent and beautiful typography.

Just imagine if all the pubs in the UK hired typographers to design their chalkboards. It would be a dream come true!
