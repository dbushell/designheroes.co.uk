Review

Paul Tebbott

Posted by David Bushell

Paul Tebbott describes himself as a "self-taught graphic designer and electronic musician".

Tebbott is based in the UK. He produces much of his work using a minimal illustration style. For a "self-taught" designer he shows commendable discipline and refinement to keep design to its necessary elements. The choice of colour is also very considered, maintaining a consistent feel across his portfolio.

You can view more work on Tebbot's flickr photostream, and keep up-to-date on twitter @paultebbott.
