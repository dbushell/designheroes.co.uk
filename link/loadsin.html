Loads.in - How fast does your webpage load?

loads.in gives you the possibility to see how fast your (or any) website loads in a real browser from over 50 locations worldwide.

This is some what of a nifty tool. Web designer will love it because it's so pretty. Functionality-wise it seems to work well. I like the progressive screenshots it gives you. It's difficult to hit multiple servers at any speed, but considering this is a free tool that's clearly by design.

It doesn't really provide much more infomation other than how slow your web host is but it still looks cool! And no doubt serves has a good 'viral app' for the parent company.
