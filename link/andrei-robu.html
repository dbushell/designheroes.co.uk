Review

Andrei Robu

Posted by David Bushell

Andrei Robu is the design director and managing partner at Acme design office in Bucharest, Romania. His work has been featured in many magazines including Computer Arts (several times). He specialising in lettering and illustration.

Andrei's typography inspired design stands out as his best work, such as the personal typeface Robu Bold Typeface (pictured above). It's an elegant and experimental bold typeface, rounded and rendered with beautiful flowing gradients.

The aim to create something complex and original is obvious but you want your viewer to decrypt your message. There are alternative recipes that viewers find themselves attracted to and as a designer, I am aware of these. Lettering design has two main purposes: to communicate a message and to create an image. The observer sees them both. It all depends on how he decides to look at them.

Andrei also runs the design blog Designers Go To Heaven which is a carefully curated inspiration archive using the Tumblr platform. And he's on twitter @andreirobu.
