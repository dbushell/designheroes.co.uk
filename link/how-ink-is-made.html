How Ink is Made

Posted by David Bushell

How Ink is Made – The Printing Ink Company of Canada have a great eight minutes on their dedication to ink production.

A Chief Ink Maker shows how colour and ink is created from the raw ingredients--powder, varnish, and passion. Everything designers and printers need to know about the process, the challenges and joy of ink making.

Perhaps it's just my isolation within web design for the last few years but this video is fascinating. There's such an art to mixing ink but will digital eventually kill the craft? That would be a shame.
