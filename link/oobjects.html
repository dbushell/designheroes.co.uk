Review

Oobjects

Posted by David Bushell

Oobjects is a list blog of man-made objects curated by web architect David Galbraith.

Oobject is a kind of online Wunderkammer comprising visual lists of man-made objects. Oobject may look like yet another, crappy, weird things site, but delve in, I’ve put an unhealthy amount of effort into it.

If you must use online galleries as a source of design "inspiration" then something a little less direct may actually open your mind. Oobjects is as unpredictable as list blogs get, and that makes is rather special.

The author David Galbraith is an interesting guy himself, I particularly like this line from his about me:

Web design still usually refers to the way things look, rather than the way they work, Web Designer being a title given to a front-end UI designer.
