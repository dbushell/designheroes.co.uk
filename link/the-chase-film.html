The Chase Film

Posted by David Bushell

The Chase Film is an advertisement for the Intel Core i5 processor.

Intel is launching an action-adventure video titled "The Chase." The spot demonstrates the performance capabilities of the new processors by creating an action-movie style chase sequence that takes place through a wide variety of program windows on a computer desktop.

The video is a clever concept and well executed although not completely original, but what is these days? It was created by agency Venables Bell & Partners.
