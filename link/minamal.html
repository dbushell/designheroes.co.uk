Review

Minamal

Posted by David Bushell

"Minamal is not a CSS gallery. It's a collection of websites, photos and videos chosen for their beauty or brilliance."

Minamal is a new website created by David Merfield that looks to be a very promising personal inspiration archive from a great designer.

I'm a strong believer in curating your own collection of inspiration. Whether you decide to publish it on a website or not it's an invaluable method of expanding your knowledge and understanding of design. It's especially useful for reminding yourself of those awesome sites you've visited, which is why Minamal is now on Design Heroes!
