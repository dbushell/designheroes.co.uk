Notes and Dispatches

Posted by David Bushell

Notes & Dispatches is a personal linkblog by Tom Bryan, an Australian designer and developer.

Notes & Despatches is a place for me to post short articles, links and photos — small scraps of content that might otherwise fall down the cracks between Twitter and my "proper" website.

I love the blog concept, it's exactly why I run Design Heroes! I think everyone should run a similar blog, something I like to call "inspiration archives".
