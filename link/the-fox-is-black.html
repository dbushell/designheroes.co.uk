Review

The Fox Is Black

Posted by David Bushell

Kitsune Noir blog re-brands as The Fox Is Black. Founder Bobby Solomon writes in his profile:

The Fox Is Black, formerly Kitsune Noir, was started in April of 2007 as a way of sharing the interesting ideas and products I came across. My friends and family were never as excited as I was about the things I would come across so this site is a way to share ideas with a wide range of people. The blog has now grown quite significantly, reaching millions of viewers a year and includes two additional contributors.

I've been an avid reader of Kitsune Noir so I look forward to seeing the same great content on the new blog. The Fox Is Black has a fairly standard and functional design, I would compare it to the old but I can't for the life of me remember what it looked like! The curse of the RSS Reader. Anyway, remember to update your RSS feed!
