Review

Stuff No One Told Me

Posted by David Bushell

Stuff No One Told Me is a blog by Spanish illustrator Alex Noriega featuring funny illustrated thoughts on life's curiosities. Alex describes himself as:

a 29 years old bunny from Barcelona ... He's also very poor, so don't make fun of him

His sense of humour carries across in an artistic style that mixes a cute hand-drawn aesthetic with a clever and often mature narrative. The captions and images both compliment one another strongly in these delightful illustrations.

You can see more of Alex's work on his personal Sketch Blog. He has also started another blog titled "Your Mother Likes it" featuring more illustrations in the same style.

Perfect to brighten up your morning.
