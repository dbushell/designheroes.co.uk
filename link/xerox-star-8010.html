Xerox Star 8010

High quality Polaroid scans of the Xerox Star 8010 GUI (graphical user interface) taken in 1981 - a few years before Apple's Macintosh, and long before Microsoft Windows. These photographs show the incredible design challenge of a revolutionary new medium.
