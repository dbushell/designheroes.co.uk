Greasemonkey VS iStock

This ingenious Firefox plugin was created by Jay Hollywood; aussie freelance designer. The handy little script allows you to access usable high resolution placeholders from iStockPhoto.com for mocking up designs. Though the image maintain their watermarks, it's still not necessarily legal, so sshhh!
