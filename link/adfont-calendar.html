Adfont Calendar

Posted by David Bushell

Adfont Calendar from Fontdeck is a clever little promotional piece from the web font service provider. It looks like they'll be giving away free annual licenses every day, how kind :)

In the spirit of advent calendars the world over, the Adfont Calendar offers a small gift every day in the run up to Christmas. Search for the day’s door and behind it you’ll find details of a great typeface, with a free webfont available free for that day only, so be sure to come back each morning for more type goodness.
