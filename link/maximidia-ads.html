Maximidia Ads

Posted by David Bushell

Advertising for the event Maximidia - a marketing conference in Brazil - by South American ad agency Moma.

More information and full size ads for Facebook, Skype and YouTube can be found over at (the awesome) adland.tv. See Ads of the World for them in more context with the strapline "Everything ages fast. Update."

The ads really poke fun at the style and aspirations of the era - at least my perception of it, I wasn't around in the 50s! But that's all that matters right? Perfect for a marketing event.
