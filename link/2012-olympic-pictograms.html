2012 Olympic Pictograms

Launched last Friday were the much anticipated London 2012 Olympic pictograms. The Olympic pictograms were made famous by the marvellous Munich '72 set, or perhaps Mexico '68 takes your fancy. Whichever set is your favourite they're a challenge to design not least due to the scrutinization of the design community. They serve as a universal language for athletes and spectators from around the world.
