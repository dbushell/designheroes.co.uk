An excellent source of British design through the decades. "The Design Council Slide Collection has its origins in the Photographic Library set up by the Council of Industrial Design (CoID) in 1945. Font Squirrel

Font Squirrel is an excellent source of free fonts. And when they say free, they actually mean free - with no legal issues for commercial-use. Most font licenses are iffy and out-dated in regards to usage, especially on the web. No longer do you have to lay awake at night worrying if the feds are about to bust you for questionable font usage. "We know how hard it is to find quality freeware that is licensed for commercial work. We've done the hard work, hand-selecting these typefaces and presenting them in an easy-to-use format."
